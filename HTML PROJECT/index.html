Iconic Zippo Lighter

The classic Zippo lighter, recognized for its dependability and unique design, has been a preferred choice since it was introduced in 1933.

In the 1990s, Zippo experienced a rise in popularity, turning into a global collector's item. Its traditional styles, exclusive releases, and its cultural importance established it as a fixture in pop culture. By the close of the 1990s, more than 300 million Zippo lighters had been sold, demonstrating its lasting attraction and dependability.

Currently, Zippo still provides a diverse selection of top-notch lighters that combine practicality with elegance. Discover their collection official Zippo Canada website.
